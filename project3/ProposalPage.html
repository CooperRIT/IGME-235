Parry Puncher

Description:

You take on the role of a lone fighter that never hits first. He must wait for the enemy to attack in order to parry and defeat his foes.

Genre

  • Brawler
  • Action

Platform

Desktop Web

Story

The character you play as loves fighting, but his master alwasy scolded him for getting into needless fights.
In order to honor his master he vowed never to be the person to start a fight, but there is a problem. He has no paitence and hates waiting for people to fight him.
So now he wanders the land provoking people only to parry their attacks and win fights.

Aesthetics

Simple Pixel art, 16bit

Gameplay

You will move about a top down dungeon waiting for enemies to attack so you can parry.
